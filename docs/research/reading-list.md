# ETR Legal Reading List & Evidence Register

A verifiable list of the sources behind ETR's services and website tools.
**All links are to legal, freely accessible versions** — open-access journals,
preprint servers (arXiv), university repositories, or author-shared copies.
No paywall-bypassing services are used or endorsed.

> Tip for closed-access papers: use **Unpaywall**, **CORE.ac.uk**, **OpenAlex**,
> **Google Scholar** ("[PDF]" link), or simply request the paper from its author —
> these are all legal ways to obtain a free copy.

### Legal & ethical note
This register deliberately uses only legitimate access routes. Paywall-bypassing
repositories (e.g. Sci-Hub) are **excluded** because distributing paywalled papers
without permission is copyright infringement in most jurisdictions and a
reputational risk for a professional firm seeking academic and industry
recognition. Every claim ETR makes publicly can be traced to a source above.
